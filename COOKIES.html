Recipe #4 Flourless Fudge Cookies
Flourless Fudge Cookies: Ingredients
2 1/2 c. powdered sugar
3/4 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
1/4 tsp. Kosher salt
4 egg whites
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 1/2 c. semisweet chocolate chips
PREP TIME: 0:05
TOTAL TIME: 0:15
Preheat oven to 350Â° and line two baking sheets with parchment paper. In a large bowl, combine powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
Stir in egg whites and vanilla then add chocolate chips. Scoop tablespoon sized cookies onto prepared baking sheets and bake until set, 10-12 minutes.
