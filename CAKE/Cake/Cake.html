FOR THE CAKE
Cooking spray, for pan
3 c. all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 c. sugar
1 1/2 c. oil
4 large eggs
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
3 c. grated carrots
1 c. pecans, roughly chopped, plus more for garnish
1 c. rasisins
FOR THE CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
1 (8-oz) block of cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. (1 stick) of butter, softened
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
4 c. powdered sugar
Directions
PREP TIME: 0:10
TOTAL TIME: 1:10
Preheat oven to 350Â° and grease 2 - 8" round cake pans with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, whisk together flour, salt, baking soda, and cinnamon.
In another large bowl with a hand mixer (or in the bowl of a stand mixer),
beat together sugar and oil until well combined.
Add eggs one at a time beating well after each addition.
Mix in dry ingredients just until combined.
Stir in carrots, walnuts, and raisins.
Divide batter evenly between prepared pans.
Bake until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean, 45 minutes.
Let cool 15 minutes then invert cakes onto a cooling rack and let cool completely.
Make frosting: In a large bowl with a hand mixer, beat together cream cheese and butter until smooth.
Add vanilla and powdered sugar and beat again until smooth and a spreadable consistency.
Frost cake as desired.

Ingredients
3 pt. coffee gelato, such as Talenti Coffee Chocolate Chip
2 c. strongly brewed coffee
1/3 c. dark rum
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
36 Ladyfinger cookies
1/4 c. unsweetened cocoa powder, for garnish
2 tbsp. chocolate curls, for garnish
Directions Set out ice cream to soften slightly, 10 minutes.
Meanwhile, in a shallow bowl, whisk together coffee, rum, and vanilla.
Transfer ice cream to a medium bowl and stir until smooth and spreadable.
In a 9"x13" pan, spread one pint of the coffee ice cream in a single layer.
Dip each lady finger in coffee mixture and lay in an even layer on top of ice cream, cutting lady fingers if needed.
Repeat with another layer of ice cream and another layer of cookies.
Finish with a layer of ice cream, cover with plastic wrap and place in refrigerator to refreeze, 3 hours up to overnight.
When ready to serve, dust with cocoa powder and sprinkle with shaved chocolate.
Run a knife under hot water and dry, then slice into squares and serve.
